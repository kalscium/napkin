=== POSSIBLE GAMEPLAY ===
* survival game, gather resources
* no hostile mobs or even man-made structures at all
* you are alone
* procedurally generated terrain (wave-function collapse) with seeds
* large worlds
* your biggest enemy is the environment
* natural disasters
* rain, corrosion
* wind, tornados
* earth-quakes
* trees fall
* lightening
* hunger, thirst and lucid-ness
* when your lucid-ness is low, the world slows down, blurs, bloom gets
  stronger, sound distorts; you know what I'm talking about
* peaceful mobs and foxes
* no end
* similar gameplay to building sandcastles at the beach and seeing how
  long they last
* you can never win, all you can do is extend how long you and your
  structures last
* there are no bugs, only features that are a direct result from the
  world's rules
* a settings page that lets you tweak the intensity of the world and
  other stuff
* local LAN multiplayer

=== POSSIBLE MUSIC & SOUND DESIGN ===
* a bunch of different rain sounds for every situation
* incredibly immersive ambiant sound
* put the MOST focus on the sound
* music, similar to that of minecraft's

=== POSSIBLE 'FOREVER' FEATURES ===
* fully open-source; make money from videos and merch
* mods compiled to WASM and run in a sandbox so they're safe and
  universal
* independant open-source server binaries for multiplayer
* no 'official' servers, but I can have servers for the youtube channel
  that makes the game

* bloom
* fog & blur
* no anti-aliasing
* low poly models
* low resolution
* soft but vibrant lighting with rain
